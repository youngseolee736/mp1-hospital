Contact details

  • Telephone: +82-31-651-1313

  • Access: From Pyeongtaek Station on Line 1, take regular bus No. 20 or 2 and get off at Seongdong Elementary School stop.

Parking information

Visitors can use the hospital parking area for convenient access to the clinic. Outpatients are entitled to 4 hours of free parking, and hospitalized patients can receive 6 hours of free parking on the day of admission and discharge.

Please inform the International Healthcare Center if you need to register your vehicle for hospital parking support.
