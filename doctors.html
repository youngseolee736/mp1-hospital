Medical staff introduction

Doctor profiles and specialist care

The doctor information below highlights the hospital’s medical staff expertise in orthopedic, hand surgery, pain medicine, and physical therapy specialties.

Dong Min Kang, M.D., Ph.D.

Spine / Artificial Joint Specialist

Graduated from Yonsei University College of Medicine and trained at Kaiser-Meyer Spine & Joint Center in Germany.

Specialty care: Lumbar & cervical spine, knee joint disease, spinal stenosis, spondylolisthesis, and osteoarthritis.

Hyung Ho Kim, M.D., Ph.D.

Upper limbs endoscope / Hand surgery specialist

Graduated from Hanyang University College of Medicine and has clinical audit experience in hand and upper limb care.

Specialty care: Carpal tunnel syndrome, trigger finger, DeQuervain syndrome, ganglion cyst, and epicondylitis.

Geun Soo Lee, M.D., Ph.D.

Endoscope / Microsurgery Specialist

Graduated from Yonsei University College of Medicine and worked in the Yonsei University Endoscopy Center.

Specialty care: Knee joint disease, meniscal tear, cruciate ligament injuries, and cervical/lumbar spine care.

Yeon Ho Kim, M.D., Ph.D.

Upper limbs endoscope specialist

Graduated from Seoul National University College of Medicine and is a regular member of the Korean Society of Shoulder Joint & Sports Medicine.

Specialty care: Shoulder joint disease, rotator cuff tears, adhesive capsulitis, recurrent dislocation, and impingement syndrome.

Go Wie Kim, M.D., Ph.D.

Foot & ankle / pediatric orthopedic specialist

Graduated from Kyung Hee University College of Medicine and is active in Korean foot, ankle, and hip joint societies.

Specialty care: Hip and foot joint disease, avascular necrosis, hip fracture, plantar fasciitis, flat foot, and scoliosis.

Won Tae Kim, M.D., Ph.D.

Pain medicine specialist

Section chief of anesthesiology and pain medicine, trained in anesthesiology and pain management.

Specialty care: Anesthetic injection, Racz, and block treatment for pain management.
