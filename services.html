Services

Orthopedic, physical therapy, and international patient care services

The hospital provides spine and joint treatment, pediatric orthopedics, rehabilitation, physical therapy, and international healthcare support for residents and visitors.

Spine & Joint Treatment

Specialized therapy for cervical and lumbar conditions, knee disease, and joint care.

  • Cervical and lumbar spine care
  • Knee, joint, and arthritis support

Pediatric Orthopedics

Care for pediatric bone, joint, and posture concerns, including hip and foot conditions.

  • Hip, foot, and posture concerns
  • Growth-related orthopedic guidance

International Healthcare Center

Translation, reservation support, U.S. military base assistance, and foreign insurance guidance.

  • Translation and appointment help
  • Foreign insurance guidance

Physical Therapy

Manual therapy, IASTM, chiropractic adjustment, pelvic floor therapy, and exercise programs.

  • Manual therapy and IASTM
  • Pelvic floor and exercise programs

Sports Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation support using exercise systems such as Keiser3020, Tower Pully, and Red Cord Sling.

  • Strength and movement recovery
  • Exercise-based rehabilitation systems

Pain Clinic

Specialist pain treatment including anesthetic injection, Racz, and block therapy.

  • Injection and block treatment
  • Pain management consultation
